Stop the Famine in Gaza!


Take Action Now!!!

Time is running out. Make your voice heard before it's too late.

WARNING: The situation in Gaza could escalate into a humanitarian disaster comparable to tragedies from history such as World War II. We cannot stand by and watch!

Why act NOW?

The famine in Gaza is not a distant problem. Tens of thousands of people, including children, are at risk of dying from hunger and deprivation. Policymakers can stop this, but only if we exert pressure.

What can you do?

  • Send letters and emails: Reach out to your parliamentarians, politicians, and international organisations. Let them know that you expect action.
  • Organise or join protests: Make your voice heard on the streets and ensure that the media pays attention to this urgent crisis.
  • Share information: Use social media, blogs and conversations to raise awareness.

Sample letter to send

Copy and adapt this letter to email your policymakers or post on social media:

			
Dear Sir/Madam,

			
The humanitarian crisis in Gaza is reaching a critical point. Due to blockades and shortages, thousands of innocent civilians, especially children, are at risk of starvation.

			
I urge you to take all possible steps to ensure immediate access to food and aid supplies to Gaza and to put an end to this humanitarian disaster.

			
We must not allow history to repeat itself. Your decisive action is now more necessary than ever.

			
Yours sincerely,

			
[Your name]
